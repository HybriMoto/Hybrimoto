About Us

HybriMoto is a next-generation mobility startup focused on designing and building smart, eco-efficient Hybrid Motorcycles from the ground up. Our mission is to deliver a cost-effective, dual-powered two-wheeler that combines the strength of Internal Combustion with the precision of electric mobility â€”perfectly suited for urban and semi-urban consumers in India. We aim to make sustainable transportation both accessible and affordable for everyday riders.

We are making AWD (All wheel Drive) Hybrid Motorcycle that is powered by Twin motors and an IC engine .Our hybrid vehicle features four distinct operating modes: ICE, EV, Assist, and Smart. A manual switch allows users to seamlessly toggle between ICE, EV, and Assist modes based on driving needs. The Smart mode automatically selects the optimal mode by analyzing driving conditions, ensuring maximum efficiency and performance without driver intervention.

Market Analysis

In the last six years, India's two-wheeler market has seen a steady rebound in ICE (petrol) motorcycle sales, from 13.6 million in Fiscal Year 2021 owing to the pandemic to approximately 19.6 million in Fiscal Year 2025. Meanwhile, EV two-wheeler sales increased from 28,000 units in Fiscal Year 2019 to over 1.1 million units in Fiscal Year 2024, representing a 33-fold increase and roughly a sixth of the market. This rapid growth of EVs, along with the persistent dominance of gasoline vehicles, suggests a big opportunity for hybrid bikes to close the performance-efficiency gap.

  • Improved Range compared to an EV vehicle
  • Better and improved mileage compared to ICE bikes
  • 4 Drive modes for city and highway
  • AWD capability for Hill assist and quicker acceleration
  • Self recharging capability in ICE mode
  • Reduced carbon emissions and an efficient Alternative
